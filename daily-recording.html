Daily Recording with MapAlbum

Published: February 9, 2025 | Category: MapAlbum Usage

Not just trips, but ordinary days are worth recording too. Beautiful scenery found during walks, new cafes discovered, flowers spotted while passing by... Recording these small discoveries on a map makes every day richer. Here's how to record daily life using MapAlbum.

1. Recording Walking Routes

Why Record Walking Routes?

Recording daily walks creates new enjoyment.

  • Capture seasonal changes in photos
  • Remember favorite paths
  • Discover new routes
  • Use as exercise records
