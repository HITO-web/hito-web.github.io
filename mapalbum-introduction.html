MapAlbum - A Web App to Preserve Memories on a Map

Published: February 9, 2025 | Category: Web App Introduction

Have you ever taken photos during a trip and later wondered, "Where did I take this?" MapAlbum is a free web application that solves this problem by allowing you to record memories by combining photos with location information.

What is MapAlbum?
A web app that lets you pin photos on a map to visually organize your memories. Perfect for travel records, favorite cafe tours, family memories, and more.

Why MapAlbum?

1. Simple and Intuitive

No complicated setup required. Just click on the map and add photos to start using it immediately. Works on both smartphones and computers with any browser.

2. Organize Memories by Location

Record location information along with photos, making it easy to see "where you did what" at a glance. Review your memories by place rather than just chronologically.

3. Privacy-First Design

Your data is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded to servers, ensuring your privacy is protected.

Recommended Uses

Travel Records

Record tourist spots and delicious restaurants with photos on the map. Great for reviewing your travel routes later or recommending spots to friends.

Cafe Tours and Gourmet Maps

Create your own gourmet map by recording favorite cafes and restaurants with photos. Use the memo function to note recommended menu items and ratings.

Family Memory Album

Record parks and playgrounds visited with family, preserving growth memories on the map. Perfect to look back on together when children grow up.

Walking Route Records

Document seasonal flowers and scenery found during daily walks. Create your own walking map and enjoy your favorite routes.

Easy to Use in 3 Steps

  1. Click on the map - Click where you want to add a photo
  2. Select a photo - Choose and upload a photo from your device
  3. Add a note - Enter a title and description (optional)

That's it! Your memories are now recorded on the map. Click on any added photo to view details anytime.

Try It Now

MapAlbum is completely free and requires no account registration. You can start using it immediately with just a browser. Why not preserve your memories on a map?
